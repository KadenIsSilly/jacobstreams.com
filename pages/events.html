The GOAT Games is a challenge series where I go against my streamer friends in a series of games to see who the GOAT gamer is!


Each person picks 3 games that they are good at as well as 3 games that are neutral to both players, leading to up to 9 possible games played. The winner is the first person to win 5 games.

Past guests include saithsfuff, KadenIsSilly & tertel.

The Shirley Awards is my yearly community awards show! At the end of each year we celebrate the year that has passed, and the people who made it what it was!


It's always one of my biggest streams of the year, with tons of special guests, physical trophies for every winner & a highly produced live broadcast! It is made possible by JStreamsBot, which supplies most of its funding.


The Shirley Awards has been running since 2022 and was originally called The Silver Awards. It has evolved over time, now consisting of guest presenters, special surprises & announcements! It always takes place at the end of December.

Jacob Jackpots (affectionately known as Jacob Jillions) is a gameshow created by me where I put my streamer friends to the test to find out if they are smarter than the average Twitch chatter.


The show consists of five main rounds, with each question in the round worth one more point than the previous round (round 1 = 1 point per question up to round 5 = 5 points per question). The points per round is calculated by how easy/difficult a test group of JacobStreams viewers found the questions ahead of time, meaning the round worth 5 points should have the hardest questions. There is also a finale round, which is only played by one player, the person with the most points after the five main rounds. In the finale the contestant plays to win a large gifted sub prize.


Rounds vary from episode to episode, and are often catered towards the participants. Previous contestants include Lukey, ItsBandorax & tinycaity. A season of the show comprises of 5 episodes, with different contestants on the first four episodes and then a winners showdown on the fifth and final episode of the season.
